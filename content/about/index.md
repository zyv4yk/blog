<h2 class="section-title" data-id="01">who i am</h2>
<div class="summary" style="max-width: 720px; color: var(--ink-2); font-size: 15px; line-height: 1.7;">
<p>I'm a backend engineer turned platform engineer — started in PHP (Symfony, Laravel, Yii) building services, then got pulled into the systems that run them.</p>
<p>These days I'm focused on the unglamorous craft of keeping deploys boring: centralised CI/CD on GitHub Actions, Helm and Istio in production, and the supply-chain and compliance work that's getting harder to ignore.</p>
<p>I write about it on this site and occasionally talk about it in person — most recent talk was <em>"Observability as Code"</em> at a backend meetup.</p>
<p>Open to talks, collaboration, and coffee.</p>
